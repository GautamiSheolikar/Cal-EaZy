Yeah, yeah, everyone builds a calculator when they start out. I tried to fight it, but the developer rite of passage got the best of me! Honestly, I'm glad it did, because I got to build Cal-EaZy.

Cal-EaZy is a simple calculator that's a whole lot of everything: standard modes, scientific modes, and a friendly local AI to rescue you from homework headaches. I built this keeping my own school struggles in mind, specifically the sheer panic of having to raise my hand for help. This is for all the quiet kids who just want to figure it out on their own terms. Built for students who hesitate to ask for help in class, it offers clear, step-by-step guidance right when they need it.

Full disclosure: the 'AI' isn't some heavy, planet-heating superpower. It's just a bunch of clever, hardcoded math methods running locally in your browser. Perfect for homework, zero carbon footprint!
